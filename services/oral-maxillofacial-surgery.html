Maxillofacial Surgery

Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery in Rohtak

When dental care shifts from routine maintenance to complex surgical intervention, you need a highly specialized clinical environment. Oral and Maxillofacial surgery tackles diseases, injuries, and defects in the head, neck, face, jaws, and oral tissues. Oriiface Dental Care provides hospital-grade oral surgical procedures directly to patients in Rohtak.

What The Procedures Involve

As a specialized surgical center, we handle advanced operatory cases including:

  • Impacted Wisdom Tooth Extraction: Surgically removing wisdom teeth that are trapped under the bone or growing at destructive angles, causing chronic pain or infection.
  • Maxillofacial Trauma: Treating facial lacerations, intra-oral lacerations, fractured facial bones, and fractured jaws resulting from accidents.
  • Pathology & Biopsies: Identifying and managing oral cysts, tumors, and severe infections.
  • Pre-prosthetic Surgery: Smoothing or reshaping the jawbone to ensure a perfect, comfortable fit for future dentures.

Who It Is Recommended For

If your general dentist has told you an extraction is "too complicated" or "too close to a nerve," you need a Maxillofacial surgeon. If you are experiencing locked jaws (TMJ), severe swelling that is affecting your breathing or swallowing, or structural facial injuries, immediate surgical intervention is strongly recommended.
