Orthodontics

Straighten Your Teeth with Orthodontics in Rohtak

Crooked and crowded teeth are harder to clean, prone to decay, and can cause jaw pain over time. Orthodontics is the specialized dental branch focused exclusively on shifting teeth into their proper, functional alignments. At Oriiface Dental Care at Delhi Bypass Chowk, we offer modern wire and wire-free solutions to perfectly align your bite.

What The Treatment Involves

Tooth movement requires sustained, gentle pressure over a period of months. The approach varies based on your needs:

  • Traditional Metal Braces: The gold standard for correcting complex bite issues. Exceptionally durable and cost-effective.
  • Ceramic Braces: Function like metal braces but use tooth-colored brackets, making them far less noticeable.
  • Clear Aligners: A modern, invisible alternative. Transparent, removable trays that incrementally shift your teeth without wires or brackets.
  • Retainers: Custom-fitted appliances worn post-treatment to lock your new smile in place permanently.

Who It Is Recommended For

Anyone experiencing crowded teeth, widely gapped teeth, overbites, underbites, or crossbites. While often associated with teenagers, modern orthodontics (especially clear aligners) is deeply popular with adults looking for a discreet way to fix their teeth. It's never too late in life to correct an improper bite.

Why Choose Oriiface Dental Care?

Moving teeth through bone is a slow biological process; it must be monitored by experts. At our Rohtak clinic, we do not cut corners. Dr. Vikramjeet ensures every orthodontic case is systematically planned with long-term stability in mind. We provide transparent timelines and regular, scheduled adjustments so your treatment stays exactly on track.
