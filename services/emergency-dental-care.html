Emergency Care

Emergency Dental Care in Rohtak

Dental emergencies never happen at a convenient time. When you are suffering from severe, unmanageable dental pain or facial trauma, waiting days for an appointment is not an option. At Oriiface Dental Care, we prioritize walk-ins and emergency cases to get you out of pain immediately.

What Qualifies as a Dental Emergency?

Call us immediately if you are experiencing any of the following:

  • Severe, Throbbing Toothache: Pain that is keeping you awake at night and isn't responding to over-the-counter painkillers.
  • Knocked-Out (Avulsed) Tooth: A permanent tooth completely knocked out requires immediate reimplantation (within 1-2 hours) to survive.
  • Facial Swelling or Abscess: A swollen jaw or a painful, pimple-like bump on your gums indicates a severe infection that can spread rapidly.
  • Broken or Fractured Tooth: Resulting from an accident or biting down on something hard, exposing the sensitive inner layers of the tooth.

What To Do Before You Arrive

If a tooth is knocked out, pick it up by the crown (never the root), rinse it gently without scrubbing, and keep it in a small container of milk or your own saliva while you travel to us. For severe swelling, apply a cold compress to the outside of your cheek.
