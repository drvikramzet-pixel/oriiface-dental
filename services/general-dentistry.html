General Dentistry

General Dentistry in Rohtak for the Whole Family

Your journey to lifelong oral health starts with the fundamentals. At Oriiface Dental Care, located conveniently at Delhi Bypass Chowk, Rohtak, we provide honest, transparent, and absolutely fundamental general dentistry. We believe that preventing a problem is always better than fixing one.

What General Dentistry Involves

Our general dentistry services cover everything you need to maintain a healthy, functioning smile. This includes:

  • Comprehensive Checkups: Thorough examinations of your teeth, gums, and oral structures to catch issues early.
  • Professional Cleanings (Scaling & Polishing): Removing hard plaque and tartar buildup that regular brushing cannot handle.
  • Digital X-Rays: Accurate, low-radiation imaging to see beyond the surface of your teeth.
  • Tooth Fillings: Strong, tooth-colored composite resins to repair cavities seamlessly.

Who It Is Recommended For

Everyone. Routine dental care isn't just for when you are in pain; it's a critical part of your overall bodily health. We recommend that children, adults, and seniors come in for a routine checkup and cleaning at least every six months. If you are experiencing mild sensitivity, bleeding gums, or hold onto stubborn bad breath, a general checkup is your first step to relief.

Why Choose Oriiface Dental Care?

Led by Dr. Vikramjeet, MDS (PGIMS Rohtak), our clinic operates on a strict principle of no-nonsense, precise care. You will never be pushed toward unnecessary procedures. We explain exactly what your oral health status is, what your options are, and perform all fillings and cleanings with state-of-the-art, properly sterilized equipment.
