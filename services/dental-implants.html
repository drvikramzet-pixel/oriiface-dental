Dental Implants

Permanent Dental Implants in Rohtak

A missing tooth is more than just an aesthetic issue—it affects how you chew, how you speak, and it slowly degrades your jawbone over time. Dental implants are currently the most advanced, natural-feeling replacement for missing teeth. At Oriiface Dental Care, located at Delhi Bypass Chowk, Rohtak, we provide surgical-grade implant placement mapped out with extreme precision.

What The Procedure Involves

A dental implant is essentially an artificial tooth root. The process requires a surgical hand and time for the body to heal:

  • Consultation & Imaging: We perform digital scans to assess the density of your jawbone and plan the exact placement angle.
  • Implant Placement: A small titanium post is surgically placed directly into the jawbone. This acts as the new "root" for your tooth.
  • Osseointegration: Over the next few months, your natural bone grows around the titanium post, locking it securely in place.
  • Abutment & Crown: Once healed, a connector (abutment) is attached to the post, and a custom-made crown that perfectly matches your surrounding teeth is seated on top.

Who It Is Recommended For

Dental implants are strongly recommended for patients who have lost one or multiple teeth due to injury, decay, or periodontal disease. Unlike bridges, implants do not require us to grind down adjacent healthy teeth. If you are struggling with loose dentures, implant-supported dentures provide a life-changing, permanent grip so you can eat and speak confidently again.

Why Choose Dr. Vikramjeet for Implants?

Because implant placement is an oral surgery, you want it performed by an actual Oral & Maxillofacial Surgeon. Dr. Vikramjeet holds his MDS (PGIMS Rohtak) explicitly in this surgical field. Precision here is non-negotiable. His advanced surgical training ensures that the implant is placed at the exact depth and trajectory needed for high long-term success rates with minimal surgical trauma.
