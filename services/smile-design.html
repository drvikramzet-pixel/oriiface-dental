Smile Design

Custom Smile Makeovers in Rohtak

A great smile changes how the world sees you, and more importantly, how you see yourself. Digital Smile Design is a comprehensive, aesthetic approach designed to create a smile that beautifully fits your facial symmetry. Based right here at Delhi Bypass Chowk, Oriiface Dental Care custom crafts smiles that look completely natural.

What The Procedure Involves

Smile Design is not a single procedure, but a tailored plan. It may involve one or a combination of the following:

  • Porcelain Veneers or Laminates: Ultra-thin ceramic shells bonded to the front of teeth to fix gaps, chips, and discoloration instantly.
  • Teeth Whitening (Bleaching): Professional-grade whitening to safely lift deep stains and brighten your baseline shade.
  • Gum Contouring: Reshaping the gum line with high precision to correct "gummy smiles" and reveal more tooth structure.
  • Tooth Reshaping: Subtle filing and contouring to smooth out jagged edges and uneven teeth.

Who It Is Recommended For

If you are embarrassed to pose for photos, or consciously hide your teeth when laughing, a smile makeover is highly recommended. It perfectly addresses heavy staining that doesn't respond to standard whitening, noticeable gaps between teeth without needing years of braces, chipped or worn-down enamel, and gummy smiles.

Why Choose Dr. Vikramjeet for Your Smile?

Aesthetic density requires both medical science and a sharp, artistic eye. A bad veneer looks bulky and unnatural. Dr. Vikramjeet values subtle, life-like restorations. By carefully factoring in your skin tone, lip line, and facial proportions, he engineers crowns and veneers that seamlessly blend in. Your new smile won't look "manufactured"—it will look like you were simply born with perfect teeth.
