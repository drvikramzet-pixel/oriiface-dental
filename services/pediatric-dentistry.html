Child Dentistry

Pediatric Dentistry in Rohtak

A child's first experiences at the dentist define how they view oral care for the rest of their lives. A frightened child becomes an adult who avoids the dentist. At Oriiface Dental Care, we specialize in gentle, patient, and highly interactive pediatric care to ensure your child feels entirely safe and comfortable.

What The Treatment Involves

Children's teeth are unique, as is their psychology. Our pediatric services include:

  • Preventative Exams & Cleanings: Gentle checkups to ensure primary teeth are coming in correctly and to catch early signs of decay.
  • Fluoride Treatments & Sealants: Protective coatings painted onto the chewing surfaces of back teeth to physically block out plaque and prevent cavities.
  • Painless Fillings: Treating 'milk teeth' cavities quickly and comfortably. Primary teeth must be preserved as they act as vital space-savers for adult teeth.
  • Habit Counseling: Compassionate advice and interventions to help children stop thumb-sucking or pacifier use before it damages their developing bite.

Who It Is Recommended For

We recommend a child's first dental visit occurs by their first birthday, or when their first tooth becomes visible. Regular 6-month checkups are vital not just for health, but to normalize the dental clinic environment for the child so they do not develop dental phobias.

Why Choose Oriiface for Your Child?

Patience is our priority. Dr. Vikramjeet and the nursing staff are trained to explain instruments and procedures in child-friendly language ("tell-show-do" technique). We avoid frightening terminology, work efficiently to suit shorter attention spans, and ensure every child leaves our clinic at Delhi Bypass Chowk smiling, with a positive association for next time.
