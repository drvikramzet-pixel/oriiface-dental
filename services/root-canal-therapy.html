Root Canal Therapy

Painless Root Canal Treatment in Rohtak

A root canal doesn't cause pain; it relieves it. When the inner pulp of a tooth becomes badly infected from deep decay or trauma, a root canal is the only way to save the natural tooth structure and eliminate the debilitating ache. At Oriiface Dental Care, at Delhi Bypass Chowk, RCT is performed rapidly and painlessly with modern anethesia.

What The Procedure Involves

Modern root canal therapy (RCT) is similar to having a routine filling, just a bit more detailed:

  • Access & Cleaning: Under local anesthesia, the infected nerve and pulp tissue are carefully removed from inside the tooth.
  • Disinfection: The hollowed inner canal is thoroughly cleaned, sanitized, and shaped to prepare for the filling material.
  • Sealing: The clean canal is filled with a rubber-like, biocompatible material called gutta-percha and structurally sealed.
  • Crowning: Because a tooth without a nerve becomes brittle over time, a protective dental crown is almost always placed over it later to restore its full biting strength.

Who It Is Recommended For

If you are experiencing severe, spontaneous throbbing pain, extreme sensitivity to hot and cold that lingers long after the stimulus is removed, or a swollen, tender bump on your gums near the painful tooth, you likely have an infected pulp. Doing nothing will lead to an abscess and eventual tooth loss. If you have these symptoms, you need an RCT as soon as possible.

Why Choose Dr. Vikramjeet for RCT?

Root canals require exact precision. Instead of simply pulling a painful tooth, saving it maintains your natural bite alignment. Dr. Vikramjeet ensures that you are entirely numb and comfortable before anything begins. By utilizing modern rotary endodontics rather than manual filing, RCTs at Oriiface are completed efficiently, properly sealed, and "done right, done once." No runarounds.
