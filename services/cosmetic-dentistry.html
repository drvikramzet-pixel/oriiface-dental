Cosmetic Dentistry

Cosmetic Dentistry in Rohtak

While general dentistry focuses on keeping your teeth healthy, cosmetic dentistry focuses on making them look exceptional. From subtle brightening to repairing noticeable chips, our aesthetic treatments at Oriiface Dental Care in Rohtak are designed to give you the confidence to smile freely again.

What The Procedures Involve

We leverage high-end dental materials to perform non-invasive, highly effective cosmetic enhancements:

  • Professional Teeth Whitening: Using clinical-grade peroxide gels activated by specialized light to safely break down years of coffee, tea, and tobacco stains in a single session.
  • Composite Bonding: A highly versatile procedure where tooth-colored resin is applied and hardened with a curing light to instantly fix chipped, cracked, or slightly gapped teeth.
  • Zirconia & Ceramic Crowns: Capping damaged teeth with metal-free, translucent crowns that perfectly mimic the light-reflecting properties of natural enamel.

Who It Is Recommended For

Cosmetic dentistry is recommended for anyone who has healthy teeth and gums but is unhappy with the visual appearance of their smile. If you have an upcoming wedding, interview, or simply wish to look younger and more vibrant, treatments like professional whitening or minor bonding offer immediate, striking results without invasive surgery.

Why Choose Oriiface Dental Care?

True cosmetic dentistry is an art form. Over-whitened teeth or heavily bonded resins look fake and unmistakable. Dr. Vikramjeet and the clinical team apply strict aesthetic principles to ensure your results look brilliant but completely natural. You walk out of our clinic at Delhi Bypass Chowk looking like the best version of yourself, not an imitation.
