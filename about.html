The Doctor

Dr. Vikramjeet - Oral Surgeon in Rohtak

Dr. Vikramjeet is one of Rohtak's most experienced dental specialists, with over 12 years in the field and a focused specialization as an MDS in Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery. He holds both a Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) and a Master of Dental Surgery (MDS (PGIMS Rohtak)), combining deep academic training with the precision that only comes from years of hands-on surgical work.

His residency at PGIMS Rohtak, one of Haryana's most respected medical institutions, was followed by a Senior Residency at Jawaharlal Nehru Medical College & Hospital (JLNMCH). Those years shaped him into the surgeon he is today, with direct exposure to complex dental and maxillofacial cases that most dentists never see.

Today, Dr. Vikramjeet consults at two of Rohtak's leading hospitals, Nobel Health City and Holy Heart Superspeciality Hospital, alongside running Oriiface Dental Care & Maxillofacial Center. This presence across multiple top-tier institutions reflects the trust placed in his clinical judgment by Rohtak's medical community.

At Oriiface, every patient gets the same level of care, whether it is a simple cleaning or a complex jaw surgery. Dr. Vikramjeet does not rush through appointments. He explains, he listens, and he treats with the kind of honesty you expect from a doctor you will see again.

For families in Rohtak and the surrounding areas, Dr. Vikramjeet is the specialist they return to, and the one they send their friends to.
